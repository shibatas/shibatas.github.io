About Me

Hello! Welcome to my website. My name is Shohei, and currently I’m living in Düsseldorf, Germany with my wife and two sons.

Everyday my main focus is to live life in the present moment. Happiness is abundant. We only have to pay attention and notice all the interesting and beautiful things that are everywhere. Success is giving my full attention to whatever I am doing, because then I will have achieved the best results that I can. I truly think it all starts there. I recommend anyone to check out Leo Babauta’s website Zen Habits or read any of Eckhart Tolle’s books. They are what inspired me to try to live my life this way.

Currently I am working on learning to code, playing jazz piano, as well as trying some traditional woodworking.

With web development, I would love to join a project and help someone or an organization in need. If you’d like to get in touch with me, please contact me here.

Anyway, I hope you’ll find something of interest here on my website, and have an awesome day!
